Horses

A horses height is measured in units known as "hands." One hand is equal to four inches.

  • Scientific Name: Equus ferus caballus
  • Average Height: 14-18 hands
  • Average Lifespan: 25-30 years
  • Habitat: Open grassy areas near water

Horses vary in a wide range of coat colors and distinctive markings. Horses are usually classified first by its coat color, before breed or sex.
